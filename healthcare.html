Operations

Robotic surgery allows for minimally invasive surgical techniques which reduces scarring, risk of infection and recovery time. In the future this will allow for a 24 hour operation cycle and may allow for improved access to healthcare. At the moment, however, implementation is on a tiny scale due to the expense.
